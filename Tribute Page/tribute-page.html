Nikola Tesla

Serbian American Inventor
10/07/1856 - 07/01/1943

Nikola Tesla's Life

  • 1859 - Born to a Serbian family in Smiljan
  • 1880 - Moves to Prague to study Philosophy
  • 1881 - Becomes Chief Electrical Engineer at Budapest Telephone Exchange
  • 1882 - Tesla goes to work for Edison!
  • 1885 - Tesla submits some of his first patents
  • 1891 - The Tesla Coil gets patented
  • 1943 - Tesla dies at the age of 86
